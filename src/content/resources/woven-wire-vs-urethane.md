The wire-vs-urethane decision comes down to four factors: open area (wire
wins), wear life (urethane usually wins), blinding resistance (urethane, and
it's not close in wet near-size duty), and cost per panel (wire). The right
answer is per-deck, not per-plant — plenty of operations run wire on top decks
and urethane where the wear and blinding live.
