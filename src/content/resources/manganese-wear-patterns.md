Your worn liners are a diagnostic report, if you know how to read them. Even
wear to the discard point means the grade and profile are matched to the duty.
Localized dishing, premature thinning at the feed or discharge end, and liners
that never work-hardened each point to a specific mismatch — and each has a fix
that costs less than another short liner life.
