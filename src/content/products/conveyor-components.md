A conveyor is only as reliable as its weakest idler. HardRok supplies the full
range of conveyor components — head and tail pulleys, idlers and rollers,
belting, belt cleaners, and self-adjusting motor bases — to keep material
moving. We help you spec the right components for your load, speed, and
material, and we stock the wear items you replace most.

The failure pattern on most conveyors is quiet and cumulative: a seized return
roller starts scalloping the belt, a worn lagging sheet lets the drive pulley
slip in the wet season, carryback builds under the tail until housekeeping
becomes a daily job. Each of those has a component-level fix that costs a
fraction of the belt it protects. Tell us what your walkdowns are finding, and
we'll help you prioritize what to replace and what to upgrade.
