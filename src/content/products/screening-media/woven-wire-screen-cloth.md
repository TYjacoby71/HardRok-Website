Woven wire is still the highest open-area, most cost-effective screen media for
most sizing. HardRok makes premium woven wire screen cloth to your exact
opening, wire diameter, and weave — including hi-carbon, oil-tempered, and
stainless — and our premium media can be made in a few days. Send your deck
size, opening, and hook type, and we'll quote made-to-spec cloth.

## Specialty weaves we supply

**OptimumWire®** double-weave screen cloth is built for heavy-impact
applications — openings from 1¼" to 5" with wire diameters from .243" to
.500", where single-weave cloth fatigues early.

**HyperSlot™** slotted screen cloth alternates crimped and straight wires for
better fines removal and reduced blinding — a strong choice when near-size
material pegs a square opening.
