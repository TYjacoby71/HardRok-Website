Manganese grade is the single biggest lever on wear-part life. HardRok supplies
premium manganese in multiple grades — standard 14%, work-hardening 18%, and
high-abrasion 22% chrome-moly — so you can match the metal to your feed. Higher
manganese isn't always better: it depends on impact vs abrasion, feed size, and
how hard the surface work-hardens in service. We help you pick the grade that
gives the lowest cost-per-ton, not just the longest single life.
