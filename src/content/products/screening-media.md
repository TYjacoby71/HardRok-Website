The right screen media is the difference between spec product and reprocessing.
HardRok supplies woven wire screen cloth, urethane and rubber media, and the
screening components that go with them — and our premium screen media can be
made in a few days. Tell us your deck size, opening, and material, and we'll
help you choose media that lasts and screens clean.

Media choice is a trade, not a default. Woven wire buys open area and tonnage;
urethane buys wear life and quiet decks; rubber shrugs off top-deck impact.
Blinding and pegging have media-level answers too, from wire diameter changes to
self-cleaning weaves. If your screens are the bottleneck — or your operators are
changing panels more often than they should — that's exactly the conversation
our team has every day.
